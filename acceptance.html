Welcome to the Jam! 💚

See the details below!

We will send you a link to a dedicated Discord server a few days before the Game Jam. We hope to meet you online soon!

Remember to set up your working area with your laptop or desktop computer and screen and all your needed cables and peripherals (don't forget your headset to join the voice chats!). If you'd prefer, you can even create a board game with your chosen tools. It's up to you (and your team)!

See you on November 11th at 17:30 (CET)!



Best,
#FemDevsMeetup team.



Important information 📅

The where, when and how.

  • Where 📍: a dedicated Discord server 🎮 (not to confuse with our #FemDevsMeetup Discord server, where our online meetups and portfolio feedback sessions take place regularly). We will send you the link a few days before the Game Jam.
  • When 🗓️: from Friday 11th of November at 17:30 to Sunday 13th of November at 19:00 with the following programme (remember it's the Central European Timezone):
    • FRIDAY 11th:
      • 17:30 - Welcome and introduction to the Fem Devs Game Jam
      • 18:15 - Marina Díez (@Ninfa_dp) will give an inspiring opening talk on the experiences and challenges of finding your own voice in the games industry
      • 19:00 - Theme reveal
      • After the theme reveal we will help you all find a team and set yourself up for jamming!

    You can continue making your game throughout both nights, but we also recommend you to take a break and sleep some hours. Code is clearer and drawings look better after some rest. 💚

    • SATURDAY 12th:
      • All day jam! 🕹️💚🍯🍓
      • From 10:00 to 19:00 there will be organisers around in case you have any questions

    • SUNDAY 13th:
      • From 10:00 until the end of the game jam there will be organisers around in case you have any questions
      • 15:00 - around this hour you should stop implementing features and prepare a build to upload to the itch.io game jam page
      • 17:30 - Twitch stream starts 📺 (streaming links tba). Show your Game Jam games! 🎮
      • 20:00 - End of the Fem Devs Game Jam
  • How 🦄:
    • we will release the secret theme at the opening.
    • You can either participate with a pre-existing group or team up on the spot. Each group will have their own chat room and voice chat. There will also be chats to chill around and a Q&A section to reach out to our organisers.
    • You will have until 17:30 on Sunday to finish your game, and then we will present the results to the rest of the jammers and friends. There's no restriction to how much you want to keep updating your game after the event is over. Hit us up if you do, we love to see your projects anytime 💚

Rules and FAQ 💡

Norms and suggestions here and there.

  • It is necessary that all attendees follow our safe space policy.
  • We welcome talent from all areas, experience is not necessary to participate.
  • You can come with a pre-existing group, yet we recommend to team up with strangers, it's part of the fun! If we find the groups unbalanced (for example: a team with 4 programmers and no artist) we might ask you to shuffle a bit to compensate.
  • You're welcome to register regardless of your gender (and it would be awesome if you could bring a female friend!).
  • Besides the theme, there will be a list of diversifiers for extra challenge. They are all optional, but they might help you on your game concept.
  • We will take screenshots to document the event. If you do not wish your avatar or user tag to appear anywhere in our promotional media, tell us then. We will also stream the games on public.
