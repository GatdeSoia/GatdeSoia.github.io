Description

13 Rosas is a horror adventure game about the nature of fascism in general and the Spanish Civil War (1936-1939) in particular.

Explore a nightmarish, impossible building to retrieve thirteen red roses to the monster guarding the exit gate. The roses lie behind puzzles and philosophical riddles inspired by texts from, among others, Umberto Eco, Antonio Gramsci and Clara Zetkin, paintings, photographies and poems from artists like Pablo Picasso, Gerda Taro and Federico GarcÃ­a Lorca, and several historiographical documentation on these dark times.

The Thirteen Roses

The Thirteen Roses were an actual group of young socialist women fighting on the Republican band, who were executed not during, but after the end of the war. The demo version will be released on their anniversary (August 5th, 2022).

The name of the Thirteen Roses trascends their own existance: what was meant to be an exemplary penalty became a symbol of the resistance until nowadays. Their presence in the game serves as a common thread for the historical memory of this country.
