Description

13 Rosas is a horror adventure game about the characteristics and mechanisms of fascism in general and the Spanish Civil War (1936-1939) in particular.

Explore a nightmarish, impossible building to retrieve thirteen red roses for the monster guarding the exit gate. The roses lie behind puzzles and philosophical riddles inspired by texts from, among others, Umberto Eco, Antonio Gramsci and Clara Zetkin, paintings, photographies and poems from artists like Pablo Picasso, Gerda Taro and Federico Garc√≠a Lorca, and several pieces of historiographical documentation dedicated to these dark times.

The Thirteen Roses

The Thirteen Roses were an actual group of young socialist women fighting on the Republican side, who were executed not during, but after the end of the war. The demo version release was on the anniversary of their assassination (August 5th, 2022).

The name of the Thirteen Roses transcends their own existence: what was meant to be an exemplary penalty became a symbol of the resistance which continues to exist to this day. The presence of these roses inside the game serves as a common thread for the historical memory of Spain.
