About Me

I'm Casilda de Zulueta, a technical artist, animator and indie game developer based in Berlin.

In the last 9 years, I have been filling the gaps between code and art, and take pride on making game assets run smoothly without sacrificing visual quality.

My industry expertise includes:

  • art direction
  • art and animation pipelines
  • shaders programming
  • graphics optimization
  • lowpoly/midpoly 3D art
  • technical animation and complex rigging
  • production planning

I practice game development with a holistic approach, where theory and praxis, artistic freedom and technical constraints, work together to uphold a cohesive design.

I am particularly fascinated by the influence of pictorial and filmic languages in interactive media, horror games and aesthetic frictions in visual representations (i.e.: anything that deviates from conformist art). My desire for challenging designs coexists with a profound appreciation for the craft and its history. I will only work with humans.
