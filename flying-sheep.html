Flying Sheep Games

HTML5 original games.

Developer: Flying Sheep Studios.

A

Between 2017 and 2020, I worked as a 3D artist for Flying Sheep Studios, a German game development company focused on HTML5 technology. In between the regular work-for-hire (consisting mainly on kid games for the giant German network Super RTL), the studio took the chance on creating their own games.

In the beginning, they served as low-time tasks to explore new formats and test technical constraints. I first started fixing environments for an existing three-lane racer (optimizing polycount and UV space and redoing the texturing to make it pop). For my last period at the company, I became the art director of a cozy farming game and one of the 3D character artists and the lead animator of a mobile MOBA.

More than a 3D artist

Who knows how to use After Effects and has two thumbs? My previous life as a video editor for TV broadcast proved handy.

Flying Sheep's marketing department consisted on artists recording ingame footage and slapping the vanity plate on top, then sharing it in the company's social media accounts. The task would usually be assigned to one of the artists involved in the development of the game (either the UI/2D artist or the 3D artist). I enjoyed having this change of tasks once in a while, especially when we were preparing more material for conferences and pitching.
