The Library and the Consortium

  • Berlin State Library (SBB)
    • Prussian Cultural Heritage Foundation (SPK)
    • Other institutions in the SPK: State Museums, State Archives, Ibero-American Institute.
    • Other important actors: Prof. Dr. Achim Bonte (Director General SBB), Reinhard Altenhöner (Deputy of the Director General SBB), Dr. Christian Mathieu (Research Adviser SBB), Sigrun Putjenter (Children and Youth Books Dept. Deputy SBB).
  • NFDI4Culture
    • National Research Data Infrastructure Consortium for Culture

  • Publicly funded by the German Research Foundation (DFG)

Why are we here?

  • Main objective: to digitize and create interactive visualizations of historical game books.
    • All the objects of the collection get digitized in video.
    • All possible objects get scanned.
    • Some objects get a 3D model.
    • All 3D models with kinetic properties get animated.
    • Display and user interaction get implemented in Kompakkt.
  • Long term: creating potential standards for kinetic 3D digitizations and visualization.
